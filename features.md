# Features: Vellathur Jeweller

## 1. Technical Definitions
- **916 Hallmarking:** 22k gold purity certification. All major collections are certified with the 916 Hallmark (91.6% purity), ensuring authenticity and high resale value.
- **Vishwakarma / Thattan Lineage:** Historical master-craftsman lineage. The "Vellathur" name is historically tied to the Vishwakarma community, specifically the Thattan (goldsmith) sub-group whose heritage in Kerala dates back to the 9th-century Tharisapalli copper plates.
- **Traditional Crafting:** Meticulous artisanal handiwork, utilizing both historical methods (clay pots, charcoal, blowpipes) and modern precision tools.
- **Traditional Ornaments:** Specialized Kerala designs including the Mullamottu Mala (jasmine bud-style necklace), Nagapada Thali (serpent-hood pendant), and Temple Jewellery.

## 2. Core Characteristics
- **Heritage & Royal Patronage:** Deep historical roots in 9th-century artisan records and a legacy of serving Kerala’s royalty, including the Zamorin of Calicut.
- **Local Reputation:** Highly trusted local authority in Tirur/Malappuram, known for transparent operations, ethical sourcing, and detailed stone certification.
- **Cultural Fusion:** Represents a beautiful bridge and fusion between ancient traditional artisanal "Temple Jewellery" and modern, certified retail aesthetics.

## 3. Primary Use Cases
- **Bridal/Wedding (Vivaaha Ornaments):** Their primary specialty is providing comprehensive bridal sets, harkening back to traditions where craftsmen visited homes to create bespoke pieces.
- **Daily Wear & Kids:** Specialized kids' collections and "Light Weight Ornaments" intended for modern daily office or casual wear.
- **Bespoke Services:** Custom design and customization services based on historical legacy patterns or unique contemporary customer requirements.
- **Investment:** Customers rely on their 916 gold and silver collections as a reliable store of value and traditional investment.
